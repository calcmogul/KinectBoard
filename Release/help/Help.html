KinectBoard, Version 1.0a

This document describes how to navigate and use the KinectBoard program.

Setting Up

Kinect

Before running this program, make sure that your Kinect motion sensor is plugged into the USB port on your computer and into a power source.


"libusb" Drivers

Once the Kinect is plugged in, it will be recognized by the computer automatically. Next, open "devmgmt.msc" and select the items which contain "XBox NUI" in their name.
